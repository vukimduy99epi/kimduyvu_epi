Summary

Kim-Duy Vu, MD, is an epidemiologist and research assistant at the National Institute of Hygiene and Epidemiology (NIHE), Hanoi, Vietnam. He applies epidemiologic methods and advanced statistical techniques to manage, analyze, and interpret health data. His research program aims to strengthen the prevention, control, and clinical management of HIV and sexually transmitted infections (STIs), with a particular focus on evaluating intervention studies and generating evidence to inform public health practice.
